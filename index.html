Mission

Helping people in Sweden engage with advanced AI responsibly.

Safe AI Sweden helps people in Sweden learn about, discuss, and contribute to AI safety, AI governance, and the responsible development of advanced AI systems.

The initiative is early-stage. Our current focus is connecting students, researchers, engineers, policymakers, and professionals across Swedish universities, industry, civil society, and public institutions.

What we do

Practical ways to learn, discuss, and contribute.

Events and discussions

Intro sessions, talks, meetups, and small-group conversations on AI safety and responsible AI.

Reading groups and courses

Beginner-friendly paper discussions and courses covering technical, governance, and policy topics.

Career and project guidance

Signposting for people exploring useful projects, study paths, skill-building, and career directions.

Research and policy conversations

Careful discussion between researchers, practitioners, public-sector voices, and policy-interested members.

Community building across Sweden

Connecting local groups and individuals who want advanced AI to be developed and used safely.

Who should join

Open to people from many backgrounds.

  • Students Explore the field, find learning paths, and meet peers.
  • Researchers Discuss open questions and connect with adjacent disciplines.
  • Engineers Learn how technical work can contribute to safer systems.
  • Policymakers and public-sector people Follow governance discussions and share institutional perspectives.
  • Professionals from adjacent fields Bring experience from security, law, strategy, education, and operations.
  • Curious newcomers Start with accessible resources and beginner-friendly conversations.
